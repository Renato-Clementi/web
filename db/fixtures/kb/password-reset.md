# Resetting your password

If you have forgotten your password or want to change it, you can reset it
yourself from the account settings without contacting support.

## Steps

1. Open **Settings** from the top-right menu.
2. Go to the **Security** tab.
3. Click **Reset password** and follow the emailed link.

The reset link expires after 30 minutes. If it expires, request a new one from
the same screen. If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder and
make sure your address is correct under **Settings → Profile**.
